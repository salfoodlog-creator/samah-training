RPE, RIR & quick tips

RPE & RIR — what they mean

Two ways to describe how hard a set felt. Use whichever clicks for you — RPE 8 and 2 RIR mean the same thing.

RPE Feels like RIR Bar speed
6 Easy 4+ Moves fast and smooth
7 Moderate 3 Moving well, slight effort
8 Challenging Target 2 Last rep noticeably slower
9 Hard 1 Grinds on the last rep
10 Max effort 0 Bar barely moves or stops. Form breaking down — you genuinely cannot do another rep.

Effort gauge

The needle shows where most of your sets should land.

RPE 6–7 / 3+ RIR — too easy
RPE 8 / 2 RIR — your target
RPE 9–10 / 0–1 RIR — too hard
